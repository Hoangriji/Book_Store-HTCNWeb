Product Overview

The Tenth Edition of Fundamentals of Software Engineering provides a comprehensive guide to the methodologies, tools, and processes required to build high-quality software systems. In an era where software governs everything from global finance to healthcare, understanding the rigorous discipline of engineering software is more critical than ever.

Ian Sommerville integrates traditional engineering principles with modern agile methods, providing students with a balanced view of the software lifecycle. Key features include updated chapters on cloud computing, security-first design, and the ethical implications of automated decision-making systems.

  • Detailed case studies on large-scale distributed systems.
  • Integration of DevOps and CI/CD pipelines in the development workflow.
  • New focus on software reliability and safety-critical systems.
